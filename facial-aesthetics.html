VISAGE · Facial Aesthetics Intelligence — demonstration build with a simulated analysis engine.
Scores in this build are generated deterministically from your photo data plus heuristic lighting/sharpness measurement — they are not a real facial-landmark analysis. The engine interface is structured so an ML landmark model can replace the simulation without UI changes.
Aesthetics are subjective, culturally variable, and only one small part of how people perceive you. This tool intentionally focuses on controllable factors — grooming, skin health, sleep, body composition, posture and photography. It is not medical advice, and no score here should be a reason for cosmetic procedures. If appearance concerns cause significant distress, please talk to a healthcare professional.
All data stays in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is uploaded to any server.
