VISAGE · Facial Aesthetics Intelligence — browser-based facial landmark analysis.
Analysis is based on browser-detected facial landmarks and photo-based geometric estimates (MediaPipe Face Landmarker, 478 points, running entirely on your device). This is not medical advice, diagnosis, or a surgical evaluation. Results are photo-based estimates and can change with lighting, angle, camera distance, lens distortion, grooming, and body composition.
Aesthetics are subjective, culturally variable, and only one small part of how people perceive you. This tool intentionally focuses on controllable factors — grooming, skin health, sleep, body composition, posture and photography. No score here should be a reason for cosmetic procedures. If appearance concerns cause significant distress, please talk to a healthcare professional.
Photos are processed locally in your browser. They are not uploaded to a server. The landmark model (~3 MB) is downloaded once from Google's model CDN; detected landmarks stay in memory, and saved reports live in your browser's localStorage.
