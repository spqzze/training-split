General notes
  • Deload every 5–6 weeks: roughly half the volume, same or slightly lighter weight.
  • Sleep 7–9 hours — the biggest recovery lever, directly affects muscle protein synthesis.
  • Creatine monohydrate, 3–5g daily. No loading or cycling needed.
  • Caffeine 200–400mg pre-workout on heavy days (Mon / Wed / Sat).
  • Protein: 210g spread across 4–5 meals, roughly 40–50g each.
  • Hydration matters — even mild dehydration measurably reduces output.
  • RIR calibration: take one set to true failure every few weeks to keep your sense of it accurate.
  • Track weekly sets per muscle group — stay in the 10–20 range shown above.
  • Once weekly weight/rep jumps start to stall, shift toward undulating or block periodization.
  • Finish every set of an exercise once and it locks in "Last" performance, PRs, and next-session guidance.
